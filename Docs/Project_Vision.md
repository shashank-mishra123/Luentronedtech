# Project Vision

## Project Name

Lunetron Learning Platform

---

## Vision Statement

To build India's most practical online learning platform that helps students
learn industry-ready skills through live classes, recorded courses, AI
assistance, internships, coding practice, and placement support.

---

## Mission

Provide affordable, high-quality technical education and help learners become
job-ready through hands-on projects and expert mentorship.

---

## Target Audience

- College Students
- Fresh Graduates
- Working Professionals
- Career Changers
- Job Seekers

---

## Problem Statement

Many students complete courses but still struggle to get jobs because they lack
practical projects, mentorship, and placement guidance.

---

## Solution

Lunetron combines structured learning, coding practice, real-world projects,
internships, mock interviews, certificates, and placement assistance in one
platform.

---

## Business Goals

- Reach 100,000 learners
- Partner with companies
- Launch 100+ courses
- Build a strong instructor community

---

## Success Metrics

- Course completion rate
- Student satisfaction
- Placement rate
- Monthly active users
- Revenue growth
